05 / 06
Voice & motion

Calm by default.

Sentence case for everything. No exclamations. No "Awesome!" Numbers are specific. Verbs are concrete. Animations are short and never bouncy — 120 to 240ms, Linear-style ease-out. The product narrates state; it never cheerleads.

Don't
"🎉 You've submitted your report!"
Do
"Report submitted — 4:32 PM"
